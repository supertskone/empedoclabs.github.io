Part I — Privacy Policy

EMPEDOC AI LLC ("we," "us," "our") operates the Phoebe mobile application ("Phoebe," "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we process it, and what rights you have regarding your information.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, age, and biological sex. This information is necessary to provide a personalized experience and to calibrate the prediction model.

1.2 Health Data (Apple HealthKit)

With your explicit permission, Phoebe reads the following data from Apple HealthKit:

  • Sleep duration and quality
  • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Resting heart rate
  • Step count and active energy
Apple HealthKit Disclosure

HealthKit data is transmitted to our servers solely for the purpose of generating migraine risk predictions. We do not use HealthKit data for advertising, marketing, or sale to third parties. HealthKit data is not stored longer than necessary to provide the prediction service and is deleted when you delete your account.

1.3 Diary Entries

Information you log manually or via voice check-in, including: headache occurrence, severity, duration, symptoms, triggers, pain location, medication taken, mood, and notes.

1.4 Location Data

With your permission, Phoebe accesses your approximate location to retrieve local atmospheric data (barometric pressure, temperature, humidity) via Apple WeatherKit. We do not track your movements or store a history of your locations. Location is used only at the moment of a weather data request.

1.5 Voice Data

When you use the Voice Check-in feature, audio is processed to extract symptom information. Audio recordings are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers after transcription is complete.

1.6 Chat Conversations

Messages you send to the Phoebe AI assistant are transmitted to our server and to a third-party AI provider (Google Gemini API) to generate responses. Chat history is stored on our server to provide conversational context. You may delete your chat history at any time from within the App.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on private, secured servers operated by EMPEDOC AI LLC. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) between the App and our servers
  • Containerized server infrastructure (Docker) with restricted access controls
  • Authentication-protected API endpoints
  • Regular security reviews and updates

While we take reasonable precautions to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your personal data only for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the services described in this policy.

Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account at any time through the App (Profile → Account Management → Request Account Deletion). Upon confirmed deletion, all of your data is permanently removed from our servers, including: account information, diary entries, health data, chat history, and any associated records. This action is irreversible.

6. Future Use of Anonymized Data

In the future, we may use anonymized and aggregated data to improve our machine learning models and prediction accuracy. If and when we implement this:

  • All data will be stripped of personally identifiable information before any processing
  • Anonymized data cannot be traced back to individual users
  • We will update this Privacy Policy before any such use begins
  • You will be notified of material changes and may opt out at that time

As of the effective date of this policy, we do not use individual user data to train or improve our ML models.
